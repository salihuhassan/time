The Ministry of Health is a government department or agency responsible for overseeing healthcare and public health initiatives within a country. Its primary goal is to ensure the well-being of citizens by promoting and protecting public health, regulating healthcare services, and formulating health policies. Ministries of Health often work to improve access to healthcare services, control the spread of diseases, manage healthcare infrastructure, and promote healthy behaviors and lifestyles.
